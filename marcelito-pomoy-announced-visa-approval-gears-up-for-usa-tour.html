Marcelito Pomoy Announced VISA Approval, Gears Up for USA Tour

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Marcelito Pomoy via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

America’s Got Talent: The Champions alum Marcelito Pomoy has recently expressed his excitement for his upcoming USA Tour. The Filipino singer also happily announced that his US VISA is already approved, finally ensuring his attendance in the concert.

Marcelito Pomoy Announces VISA Approval

In a recent post on Instagram, Pomoy was thrilled to inform his followers that he has already processed his US VISA. Fortunately, the same has been approved, just in time for Pinoy Pride US Tour 2023.

“US VISA APPROVED!!! See you soon USA!!! Excited much!!!” he wrote.

Titled Joel Sebag and the Champions, the tour will involve inspirational pianist Joel Sebag. The musician’s keyboard expertise is expected to be highlighted by fellow Filipino singers including Pomoy, Klarisse de Guzman, and Michael Yonting.

“Known for his singular, unique, dynamic vocal range, Marcelito has wowed audiences all over the world, with his breathtaking ability to sing in both tenor and soprano,” the caption for the tour announcement reads.

For those who may not know, Pomoy was the grand winner of Pilipinas Got Talent 2011 and the third runner-up America’s Got Talent The Champions 2019. Popular for his Celine Dion renditions, Pomoy was lucky to have met Dion herself. He also appeared on The Ellen DeGeneres Show before.

He is Set to Embark on Tour Starting This Month

Allegro Musicale & Entertainment will be presenting the US tour. Additionally, Pomoy will hit the road on May 20 at The Sycuan Casino and Resort in El Cajon, California. From there, they will explore US and perform until June.

“See you soon USA!!!” Pomoy wrote.

Joel Sebag and the Champions Tour is a nine-date concert which will end on June 17, at the Diamon Jo Casino in Northwood, Iowa. Before it does so, the Filipino musicians will make stops in Temecula, California; Memphis, Tennessee; Orlando, Florida; and Dallas, Texas. They will also do shows in York, Pennsylvania; San Francisco, California; and Chicago, Illinois.
