America’s Got Talent judge Heidi Klum was a guest on The Jennifer Hudson Show this week, and she played a fun game with the host called “Ja or Ne.” That translates from German to “Yes or No.” During the game, Klum talked about the possibility of having another baby.

Heidi Klum Talks About Having Another Baby

When asked if she would have another baby, Klum asked Hudson how she felt about it. The host replied, “No, thank you.” She added, “I collect other people’s children. I’ll take your kids. But nah, ain’t trying to have no more babies. I love the baby I got.”

Klum switched her paddle back and forth between the “Ja” and “Ne” sides, saying how she feels about the idea “depends what day it is.” The 49-year-old reminded everyone that she’s given birth to four children, adding, “And then I breasted eight months each time, and then I was pregnant again.”

“Three times in a row. I have an 18, a 17, a 16, and a 13 … Now I waited a long time, so maybe ja,” she said, ultimately turning her paddle around to the “Ja” side.

Klum is currently married to musician Tom Kaulitz. The couple tied the knot in 2019, but they have no children together. Klum has three biological children with her ex-husband Seal, who also adopted her oldest daughter Leni.

Klum Teaches Hudson How to Yodel

The game ended with the question, “Would you ever teach me how to yodel?” Klum has discussed her talent for yodeling in the past, and she’s even demonstrated it on a few occasions. She was happy to give Hudson a quick lesson.

“You know, a lot of people have asked me, but no one could do it,” Klum told Hudson to start. “But you are, like, a real singer.”

She went on to demonstrate, impressing Hudson with her pipes. The host tried to imitate the sounds she was making. She was a little overwhelmed at first, but she gave it her best shot and told Klum, “You can sing!”
