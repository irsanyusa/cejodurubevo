Pop star parties with girlfriend Freda Francis, celebs at all white party [Photos]

In celebration of his new age, the ‘’ singer organized an all-white party and invited his celebrity friends.

The ‘all-white’ party was held at Tickle Bay, Lekki, Lagos. The birthday boy partied all night long with his girlfriend Freda Francis, and celebrities.

Other celebrities who showed up at the party were Ubi Franklin, 2face Idibia, Seyi Law, E-Money, 5 Star Kcee, Harrysong and Skiibii, Sauti Sol, Mike Ezeruonye, Selebobo, Tekno, Sound Sultan and Swanky Jerry among others.
