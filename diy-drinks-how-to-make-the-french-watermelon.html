How to make the French Watermelon

The French Watermelon is no different with its interesting mix of watermelon, vodka and the winner, mint.

What you need:

  • 1 large watermelon, cubed
  • 1 cup vodka
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 15 fresh mint leaves, chopped

ADVERTISEMENT

Directions:

Toss the watermelon, vodka, sugar, and mint together in a large bowl. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate 1 to 2 hours.
