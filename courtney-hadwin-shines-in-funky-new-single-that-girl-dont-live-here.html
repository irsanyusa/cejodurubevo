Courtney Hadwin Shines in Funky New Single 'That Girl Dont Live Here'

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Courtney Hadwin via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

America’s Got Talent alum Courtney Hadwin is evidently in the mood to release new music this year. Soon after her debut single, the musician dropped another song titled “That Girl Don’t Live Here.”

Courtney Hadwin Releases New Single, Official Music Video

On March 27, Courtney Hadwin surprised fans on Instagram when she announced that she’s set to release another song. “That Girl Don’t Live Here” is an immediate follow-up to the singer’s recent single “Breakable.”

“THAT GIRL DON’T LIVE HERE” OUT NOW!!!” she wrote.

Ahead of its official release, Hadwin had been treating her fans with several snaps related to the creation of the single. In one concept of her photoshoot, she was wearing a what looked like a doll or maid outfit in gingham. Meanwhile, in another, Hadwin can be seen rocking a floral pantsuit.

Later, she released the official music video of the song, showing her funky and cool music style. The video, as of writing, has reached over 32,000 views on YouTube.

‘That Girl Don’t Live Here’ Shows the Raw, Carefree Her

In the comment section of her social media posts, fans have expressed how proud they are of Hadwin. Indeed, the AGT alum has developed a more mature vocals since her stint in the series, but she’s nonetheless never lost her distinct style.

“That girl doesn’t live there anymore but she didn’t take her dance moves with her. It’s so great to see you with a microphone in your hands and moving to the music again having fun,” one user wrote.

The single screams growth and independence, embodying a girl who’s already done with the toxicity of her past relationship. Like her debut single though, it’s manifest that Hadwin wants liberty and self-determination.

“Courtney’s vocals and that Bass Line put a exclamation point onto this Crackin’ video,” another stated.

“That Girl Don’t Live Here” is now streaming on all platforms.
