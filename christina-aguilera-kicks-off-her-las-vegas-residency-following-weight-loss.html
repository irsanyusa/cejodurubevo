Former The Voice coach Christina Aguilera recently kicked off her Las Vegas residency, and some have taken note of her apparent weight loss. The pop star reportedly lost over 40 pounds.

Christina Aguilera Kicks Off Las Vegas Residency

On Saturday night, Aguilera kicked off her new Las Vegas residency at The Voltaire at The Venetian. She wore a variety of flashy, form-fitting outfits, leading some to notice her apparent weight loss. According to In Touch, Aguilera has lost over 40 pounds.

The singer reportedly lost the weight thanks to healthy eating and exercise. Aguilera had previously lost weight after giving birth to her son Max in 2008 by following a strict diet of 1,600 calories a day. She reportedly gained weight again after her divorce from ex-husband Jordan Bratman.

Now, Aguilera is reportedly done with dieting and instead has started focusing on clean eating. The star has spoken out about how she’s viewed her body in the years since she became famous, telling Health in 2021 that she “hated being super skinny” when she first started out.

“Once I turned 21, I started filling out a little bit, and I was loving my new curves,” Aguilera shared.

How Long Does Aguilera’s Residency Run?

This is Aguilera’s second residency in Las Vegas, having previously performed in 2019 and 2020 at Planet Hollywood. According to Rolling Stone, that run lasted 10 months. This time, Aguilera’s residency at The Venetian is scheduled for 10 dates.

The final date on the list is March 2. According to People, Aguilera changes her costume at least nine times in the 90-minute show, as she performs some of the biggest hits from throughout her music career.

“What I love about the intimacy at Voltaire at The Venetian Resort is how up-close-and-personal I can be with the audience… a truly modern twist on the performance experience,” Aguilera said in a statement.
