America’s Got Talent: All-Stars returned on Monday night for a new episode, as 10 more acts took the stage for a chance to go to the Finals. During the show, judge Heidi Klum hit the Golden Buzzer for young BGT magician Aidan McCann, while the Superfans voted for Pilipinas Got Talent winners Power Duo.

Heidi Klum Hits AGT: All-Stars Golden Buzzer for Aidan McCann

This week, it was Klum’s turn to hit the Golden Buzzer for her favorite act of the night. You might have expected her to give the honor to Cristina Rae, the singer who previously earned Klum’s Golden Buzzer in Season 15.

Cristina returned to the AGT stage to deliver a powerhouse performance of “Hold My Hand” by Lady Gaga. Afterward, Klum told Cristina that she didn’t think the singer needed anyone to hold her hand this time. Unlike Terry Crews, who hit his Golden Buzzer a second time for Detroit Youth Choir, Klum saved the honor for another act.

Later in the show, BGT Series 14 finalist Aidan McCann took the stage. The 13-year-old magician had help from the judges and host for his mind-reading trick. They colored in a drawing of a superhero and chose a name seemingly at random. Aidan then revealed that both he and Crews were wearing outfits that matched their choices.

“I don’t know how you did it. You are incredible. I love the magic, but really it is you who is the magic. You really are,” Klum told Aidan before hitting the Golden Buzzer to send him straight to the AGT: All-Stars Finals. She then ran on stage and gave him a hug.

Power Duo Is Going to the Finals

It was up to the Superfans to determine which of the remaining nine acts should go to the Finals. The Top 3 turned out to be Filipino couple Power Duo, Romanian dancer Darius Mabda, and AGT Season 14 contestants Ndlovu Youth Choir.

Host Terry Crews announced that Power Duo had earned the Superfans’ vote. The couple previously won Pilipinas Got Talent, where they confessed their love for each other. They later got married and welcomed a baby son. During this week’s episode, they showed off a mesmerizing routine that combined dance, acrobatics, and aerial skills.

Power Duo is now the first act in the AGT: All-Stars Finals who did not appear on a previous season of America’s Got Talent. They beat well-known AGT acts such as the Sacred Riana, Robert Finley, and Human Fountains.
