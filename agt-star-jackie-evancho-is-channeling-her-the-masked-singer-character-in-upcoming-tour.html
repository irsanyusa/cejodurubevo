'AGT' Star Jackie Evancho is Channeling Her 'The Masked Singer' Character in Upcoming Tour

FOX

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Alongside promoting her latest album, Jackie Evancho is currently busying herself with touring commitments. As she kicks off her concert series, she plans to channel her inner Kitty, her character from The Masked Singer season three.

Jackie Evancho to Revive The Masked Singer’s Kitty on Tour

Jackie Evancho recently shared a giant throwback to her time on The Masked Singer. While rehearsing for her upcoming tour, the AGT star revealed that she’ll be reconnecting with her character from The Masked Singer for the tour. In her Instagram post, the classical crossover singer can be seen singing and moving to “Diamonds Are a Girl’s Best Friend” by Marilyn Monroe amid rehearsals.

“Working on my show and decided to bring back a little taste of #thekitty to go along with my musical past present and future,” she wrote as caption.

To those who may not know, “Diamonds Are a Girl’s Best Friend” was one of her performances on The Masked Singer. Back then, fans ultimately loved this performance of hers. Hence, it isn’t surprising that Evancho chose to revive Kitty through the song.

Jackie Evancho Goes on Tour This October

Fans can once again see Jackie Evancho perform on stage as she tours in multiple City Winery locations. Her tour has recently began on Saturday, October 1 in Nashville, Tennessee, and it will end on October 28, in Atlanta, Georgia.

Evancho’s concert series is about “honoring the timeless music of Joni Mitchell,” as stated by the restaurant’s website. Essentially, she will enliven Mitchell’s hits and earliest classics, including “Both Sides Now” and “Blue.”

Tickets are still available in respective City Winery websites. As for the seats, audiences can choose between Stage Premier, Premier, Mezzanine Premier, Orchestra, and Mezzanine. The price ranges from $50 to $65.

What Has She Been Up To?

Jackie Evancho has been performing in various events lately. Last June, she performed for ESI Summit Conference in Houston, Texas. She also did an indoor concert in Ocean City, New Jersey.

Today, alongside touring, she is consistently promoting her newest album, Carousel Of Time. Before this album was issued, she was already releasing some singles in the past.

From impressing everyone with her dazzling voice in America’s Got Talent to showcasing creative maturity in The Masked Singer, Evancho is sure a star. As she continues to navigate in the music industry, she has definitely come a long way.
