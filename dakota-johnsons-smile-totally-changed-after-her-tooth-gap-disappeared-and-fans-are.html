Dakota Johnson's Smile Totally Changed After Her Tooth Gap Disappeared And Fans Are Confused

She's currently doing press for her new film The Peanut Butter Falcon, and fans quickly noticed that the gap between her two front teeth had disappeared.

People thought Dakota, 29, had it filled or fixed, but it was actually due to orthodontia, she said. (Stars...they're just like us.) She was on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon last night, and she explained what really went down.

Well, so I had a permanent retainer since I was like 13, and it was just glued to the back of my teeth, she told Jimmy. I was having a lot of neck problems recently so my orthodontist, she decided that it would be a good idea to take it off and see if my jaw sort of expanded and it helped me.
