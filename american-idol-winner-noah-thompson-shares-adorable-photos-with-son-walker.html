American Idol Season 20 winner Noah Thompson recently took to Instagram to share a series of sweet photos featuring him and his son Walker. Some of Thompson’s competitors from the show commented on how the cute the new snaps are.

American Idol Winner Noah Thompson Posts Photos with Son

In photos Thompson posted on Monday, he and Walker pose together in a field. In some photos, he holds Walker. In another shot, Walker rides on his shoulders. There’s even a photo of a heart-shaped necklace Thompson wears that has Walker’s name on it.

“Look how pretty this little dude is,” Thompson wrote to caption the post, thanking photographer Lena Webb for taking the photos. He told Webb that she “absolutely killed these.”

Some of Thompson’s American Idol co-stars commented on the post, including fellow finalist Leah Marlene, who wrote, “WALKERRRRR.” Fellow Season 20 singer Ava Maybee wrote, “My HEART.” Olivia Faye, who performed a duet with Thompson during Hollywood Week, shared three heart emojis.

Is Thompson Still with Walker’s Mother Angel Dixon?

During his time on American Idol, Thompson was in a relationship with Walker’s mother, Angel Dixon. However, in the months since, fans have speculated that he and Dixon might have broken up. There were even rumors that he might be dating Idol runner-up HunterGirl.

Fans noticed that Thompson and Dixon had unfollowed each other on social media, and Thompson had deleted photos of Dixon from his Instagram. Thompson also shared in an interview that the couple “had a hard time” adjusting to life after the show. However, Thompson hasn’t confirmed a breakup.

Recently, the singer has been playing shows around the country with HunterGirl. In October, Thompson released a new song called “Make You Rich,” along with a music video featuring him and Walker filmed in his hometown of Louisa, Kentucky.
