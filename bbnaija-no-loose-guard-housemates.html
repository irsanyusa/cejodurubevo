BBNaija No Loose Guard housemates

Tagged BBNaija: No Loose Guard, the live premiere show saw performances from top Nigerian artists like Niniola.

Meanwhile, Ebuka introduced viewers to the new housemates ready to take us on a 71-day journey of pure entertainment and drama.

1. Chinwe Elibe and Zion Ogiefa — Zinwe

Chinwe Elibe, 30, from Imo State, is bringing her vibrant energy to the BBNaija house. A graduate of Igbinedion University Okada, Chinwe enjoys dancing, playing games, partying, shopping, travelling, fashion and beauty, and making money. Chinwe enters the house with her romantic partner.

Zion Ogiefa, 27, from Edo State, is all set to make his mark in the BBNaija house. A graduate of Trinity University of Asia, Zion loves playing sports, working out, partying, hanging with the guys, fashion, and modeling.

With his romantic partner, Chinwe, Zion is ready for an unforgettable experience. They are in a five-month relationship and have been fans of the show since their teenage years.

2. Femi David and Michky — Radicals

Meet Femi David, a 29-year-old dynamo from Delta/Warri, who is ready to light up the Big Brother Naija house. A graduate of the University of Abuja, Femi's interests range from dancing and hiking to football, video games, and content creation. Follow his journey as he showcases his vibrant personality and strategic gameplay. Femi enters the house with his dance partner.

The other part of the pair is Michky the Great, a 24-year-old dancing sensation from Edo State. A graduate of the University of Calabar, Michky combines his passion for dancing with a dedication to fitness. His social media pages are full of energetic and inspiring content.

With his dance partner, David, Michky is set to bring unmatched rhythm and excitement to the BBNaija house. Interestingly, they are gym bros who take pride in the fact that they are virgins.

3. Flora Chiedo and Ruth Akpan — Floruish

Flora Chiedo, 26, from Imo State, is set to bring her vibrant personality to the Big Brother Naija house. A graduate of Knutsford University, Ghana, Flora loves partying, listening to music, and eating. She enters the house with her friend of 10 years.

Ruth Akpan, 32, from Akwa Ibom State, is ready to showcase her diverse interests in the BBNaija house. Also a graduate of Knutsford University, Ghana, Ruth enjoys cooking, playing pool, and travelling.

With her long-time friend, Chiedo, Ruth is excited to bring their dynamic to the BBNaija stage. These friends, who have shared a decade-long friendship, are ready to make their mark on the BBNaija house, bringing a mix of fun, culinary skills, and travel stories.

Joseph Bassey Christopher, 24, from Akwa Ibom, is set to charm the Big Brother Naija house. A student at Mountain Top University, Joseph's interests include hosting, running, public speaking, and designing clothes. Joseph enters the house with his colleague.

Samuel Osuji, 23, from Imo State, is ready to showcase his talents in the BBNaija house. A student at Babcock University, Samuel loves American football, boxing, basketball, drawing, and dancing.
