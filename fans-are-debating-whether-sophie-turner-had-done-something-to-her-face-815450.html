Amidst the Divorce Drama, People Are Asking: Has Sophie Turner Done Something to Her Face?

Over the last few weeks, Sophie Turner’s been making headlines because of her unexpected split from singer Joe Jonas. But among her fans, there’s also been chatter about something different they’ve noticed in her looks.

Their divorce news left everyone speechless.

Joe Jonas, 34, and Sophie Turner, 27, have just called it quits after being together for seven years, including four years of marriage. The couple, who have two kids, dropped a bombshell on everyone with their divorce announcement. Surprisingly, it was Joe who kicked off the legal process.

It came out that Joe Jonas had filed for divorce, saying that “the marriage between the parties is irretrievably broken.” However, it was decided that “the best interests of the minor children require the parties to have shared parental responsibility.”

According to one source, Joe and Sophie’s different lifestyles played a role in their decision to divorce. The source mentioned that “Sophie likes to party, while Joe prefers to stay home. Their contrasting ways of life contributed to Joe’s choice to end the marriage.”

People have been talking and wondering about how Sophie looks different.

Richard Shotwell/Invision/AP/East News

Sophie Turner was just a kid when she started on the show and has since grown into an amazing woman and mom. But recently, people on Twitter started talking about her looks.

Some thought she had plastic surgery, maybe buccal fat removal because her face seemed slimmer. These rumors started in 2022 and continued into 2023, with fans sharing their thoughts online. Some said the alleged procedure made her look older, while others preferred her with fuller cheeks.

On the other hand, some people believed Sophie’s change was just a result of getting older and losing weight. They thought her face naturally slimmed down, giving her more defined cheekbones. One tweet summed up this viewpoint: “Sophie Turner has always been skinny. When she was introduced to the world, she was a CHILD with baby fat on her cheeks still. Now she’s a woman who has gone through puberty, pregnancy, and all the body changes that go with it. Why would you assume buccal fat removal?”

She had a tough time accepting her beauty before becoming the strong woman she is today.

Lots of fans adore Sophie for who she is, regardless of any perceived changes. But in the past, Turner admitted she had a tough time dealing with how people online would trash-talk her appearance, especially when she was younger. Comments about her weight and skin really got to her.

Thus, in 2022, Turner took a break from Instagram and tried to distance herself from social media. Turns out, it was a good move for her well-being.

But as the years rolled on, Turner started feeling more confident. She got some helpful advice from her older sister-in-law, Priyanka Chopra, who told her that to get the reactions she wanted from people, she had to strut into a room with confidence and act like she owned the place.

She also gave credit to her now ex-husband, Jonas, for helping her through some really tough times. When they first met, she was mentally struggling, and Jonas laid it out straight—he couldn’t be with her unless she loved herself first. He told her, “I can’t see you love me more than you love yourself.”

However, in the middle of all this talk, it’s worth remembering the importance of self-love and embracing our natural selves. Whether or not Sophie had any procedures, the most important thing is that she feels good and confident in her own skin. Therefore, Turner is not just a talented actor, but also a role model for staying true to yourself in the face of public opinion.

Despite all the rumors and speculations, Sophie faced life’s challenges with resilience, much like she did when giving birth.
