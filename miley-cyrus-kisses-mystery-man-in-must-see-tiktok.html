Does Miley Cyrus have a new man in her life? The “Plastic Hearts” singer and former coach of The Voice posted a TikTok video kissing someone new and fans are seriously freaking out.

Miley Cyrus Kisses Popular TikTok Creator in New Video

In the TikTok video, Cyrus sings along to “Without You” by The Kid Laroi. In the background are screenshots of articles that delve into her relationship history with both men and women. Some of the articles are about her former flame Nick Jonas, drunk dialing exes, and asking a woman out through TikTok.

Midway through lip-synching the song, the sound changes to Cyrus actually singing. This new TikTok sound is called “Miley Cyrus Without You Remix” and people are already using it as the soundtrack to their own videos. In just an hour, her video garnered over 416,000 views.

Fans who stuck around to the end of the video, were shocked to see Cyrus then share a kiss with TikTok creator KingMoxu. KingMoxu has over three million followers on the platform and is notoriously known for taking his shirt off in his comedic videos.

So are Cyrus and KingMoxu an item? Since he is always trolling fans on TikTok we are going to have to go ahead and guess no. In case you need further proof that KingMoxu likes to pull off (and be involved in) pranks, just check out his many TikTok videos. While we don’t yet know how the two met and got together, nothing can really surprise us anymore.

There Was Actually a Hidden Meaning Behind the Video

Though it may seem like Cyrus posted this TikTok to stir up relationship rumors, she actually had another motive. Affinity Magazine reported that this TikTok confirms that she will be featured on the official remix of “Without You” with Laroi.

At the end of March, Cyrus posed for Instagram pictures in the recording studio with Laroi. This led fans to think that the pair were working on something together. Now, we finally know what it is.

Laroi even posted a reaction video to Cyrus’s TikTok in which he nods his head along to the song. When it came to watching the part of the video where Cyrus kissed KingMoxu though, even Laroi was shocked and a little bit confused.

“WTF did I just watch,” he captioned his TikTok.

Though there is not yet an official release date set for this collaboration, Laroi is having a whirlwind start to his career. Just last year, his song “GO” with Juice Wrld topped Justin Bieber on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100.

He also became the youngest Australian artist with a number one album on the ARIA charts with “F*ck Love” last summer. Now, working with Cyrus on this collaboration is another career highlight to tack onto his already impressive belt.
