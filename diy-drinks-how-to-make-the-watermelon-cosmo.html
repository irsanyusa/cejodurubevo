How to make the Watermelon Cosmo

What you need:

2 cups cubed seeded watermelon

1 jigger vodka

ADVERTISEMENT

4 cubes ice, or as needed

1 dash lime juice (optional)

Directions:

Blend watermelon in a blender until smooth; strain through a fine mesh strainer and discard pulp. Pour strained watermelon juice, and vodka into a cocktail shaker filled with ice; strain into a glass. Squeeze lime juice into the cocktail.
