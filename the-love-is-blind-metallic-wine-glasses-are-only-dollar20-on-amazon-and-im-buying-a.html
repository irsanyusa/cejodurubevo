The 'Love Is Blind' Metallic Wine Glasses Are Only $20 On Amazon, And I'm Buying A Whole Set

It seems like wine or some sort of libation made an appearance in every scene, but Chris Coelen, one of the show's creators, told OpragMag.com that despite the Love Is Blind contestants' many imbibing sessions, no one was ever pressured to drink. "We wanted them to make the decisions and them to control their destiny. It wasn't like, 'Hey, guys! Cocktail party time.' Whatever you wanna do, do it," Coelen explained to the publication.

The wine glasses have caught the attention of many a Love is Blind viewer on Twitter, and some have pretty strong opinions on the glassware choice. "The strongest commitment on Love Is Blind is Netflix's commitment to those metallic wine and champagne glasses," wrote one fan.
